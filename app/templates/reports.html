Reports

From this page you can download observation date PDF reports for each sensor. These reports provide an overview of the acquisitions for a particular period (e.g., annual or quarter). They are intend to allow the user to easily identify dates which might have useful imagery and while also allowing an overall understanding of the available data to be gained.
